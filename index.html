Catch Up With: Denise Williams

Past Experience, Present Projects, and Future Pursuits

What Can I Do?

I'm the kind of person who thrives with new challenges and room to grow and help others. A recently taken personality quiz stated that I have many interests, and therefore am likely to have a variety of talents. My past roles include (but are not limited to): barista, flight attendant, travel agent, promotional model, bartender, and General Manager. Each role came with a whole new set of challenges and skill sets. These have led me to learn Excel, Microsoft Office, and a variety of POS systems like Aloha and Square. Past coworkers and employers would say that I am proficient in leadership, teamwork, communication, adaptability, and I have a strong work ethic.

What Am I Up To?

Currently enrolled in LaunchCode's CoderGirl program on the Java track, I'm learning to code! I'm not yet halfway through the program but I am able to say that I can solve simple technical questions in Javascript and am able to start on some passion projects. Networking within the tech community has been a breath of fresh air as I find it to be extremely welcoming and open. This program is making what I thought to be a possibly difficult career transition into a very fulfilling one. Codergirl has brought out some problem solving skills I didn't know I had in me, as well as helped me develop my organization and time management.

The 2020 Vision

The original goal was to get out of the hamster wheel of all I knew in the workforce and make the full leap into a technical career that would grant me the freedom and satisfaction of a job well done. This has developed into the realization that this opportunity will open even more doors. By this time next year, I see myself in a full time Java developer position at a company that I love and appreciate and I plan to have launched my own blog with an adjoining app and coaching practice. The possiblities are endless!
